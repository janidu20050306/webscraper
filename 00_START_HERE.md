## COMMON MISTAKES (Avoid These)

❌ **Mistake 1:** Building perfectly instead of launching
- You'll spend 1 month building perfect scraper
- Meanwhile, others are getting customers
- Solution: 80/20 rule - launch when it works, improve later

❌ **Mistake 2:** Not talking to customers before building
- Build something nobody wants
- Solution: Validate with 5-10 customers first

❌ **Mistake 3:** Giving up after 1-2 weeks
- First customer usually takes 2-3 weeks
- Solution: Commit to 4-week sprint minimum

❌ **Mistake 4:** Being too shy to reach out
- Talking to 100 people is normal
- Most will ignore you (that's fine)
- Solution: Send more messages

❌ **Mistake 5:** Terrible margins
- Charging $50/week when you could charge $250/week
- Solution: Look at what others charge, price accordingly
